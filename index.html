One-off setup on iPhone

To get reminders on your iPhone you need to add this page to your Home Screen first. Then open it from there.

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari ⬆︎
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  3. Tap Add in the top right
  4. Open the new Stretch Goals icon from your Home Screen
  5. Then tap Turn on reminders
